Here is the page structure!

Header: Just the top of the page.
Nav: A place to put links to an off-website tab, or just links that take one to a part of the website.
Main: A thesis, a claim, a place to put what the website's all about and stuff. An image usually would go here.
Article: A place to put images/text that relates to the reason why you typed your website for.
Section: A place to put what you think is a major part of your website.
Aside: A place to put what you think is a major part of your website, just that it is in the side. Most likely a picture.
Footer: The bottom of your website's page. It could be copyright notices or social media links, whatever is considered misc. to your actual website's purpose.
